Drought-proofing

This is at the top of the list for a reason, especially in older homes with old windows and doors. Poorly drought-proofed home will waste a lot of energy trying to heat up a home, during cold and windy season. Cheapest option is to DIY jobs, just by buying and installing window drought stopper seals around all windows, you will make your home more energy efficient.
